Take the first step towards laying down a foundation for learning.

At Milestones Academy, we are passionate about giving children the best possible start in life. We believe that children learn best in a quality environment that is happy, nurturing, engaging, and safe. This is why we provide an environment that is responsive to our children’s capabilities in order for every child to experience feelings of success and accomplishment.

Nursery/Infant

From six weeks old through 12 months old. In addition to provide quality daily care, our educators implement developmentally appropriate learning activities focusing on the six learning domains of development for infants.

Toddler

From 12 to 36 months old. At this age, our educators pay special attention to fostering language and communication, as well as developing executive function skills in order to lay the foundation for preschool readiness.

Preschool

From 3 years old and up, we focus on preparing children for their elementary school years by building foundational skills in literacy, math, and problem-solving through activities that promote curiosity, critical thinking and a love of learning.

Curriculum

At our early learning center, we provide a nurturing, safe and secure environment where children can thrive, explore, and grow. Our carefully designed curriculum fosters creativity, critical thinking, and personal growth through a balanced mix of hands-on learning and play. We offer enriching extra-curricular classes in art, dance, STEM, foreign languages, and music, all included in the weekly tuition. This well-rounded approach ensures that each child receives a holistic education, nurturing their imagination, problem-solving skills, and cultural awareness, while preparing them for future success.

Our facilities

At Milestones Academy, we cultivate a warm and welcoming environment where children explore their imagination and creativity, all while feeling the comfort of a loving family home. We have a wide range of educational toys, as well as a variety of craft supplies and learning materials designed to inspire hands-on activities and foster a love for learning. We also have a spacious backyard filled with toys, providing plenty of room for children to play and enjoy the outdoors.

Nutrition

We prioritize your childrens' health by providing nutritious, fresh and balanced meals that support their growth and development. With proper nutrition being essential to a child's developing brain, we're committed to providing non-processed meals with organic ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. We take great care to meet any special dietary requirements including gluten-, peanut- and lactose-free options while encouraging awareness of the immediate and long-term effects of healthy eating.
